Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 15, 2026

1. Information We Collect

Biometric Data (Calculator)

When you use our macro calculator, you enter:

  • Age
  • Weight
  • Height
  • Gender
  • Activity level

Important: This data is processed entirely in your browser. We do NOT store, transmit, or save this information on our servers.

Purchase Information

When you buy a meal plan, we collect:

  • Email address (for PDF delivery)
  • Payment information (processed securely by Stripe—we never see your card details)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Calculator data: Used only for real-time calculation in your browser
  • Email: To send your purchased meal plan PDFs and order confirmations
  • Payment info: Processed by Stripe (PCI-compliant payment processor)

3. Data Storage & Security

We use industry-standard security measures:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for all data transmission
  • Secure cloud storage for purchase records
  • No storage of biometric data (calculator inputs are browser-only)

4. Third-Party Services

We use:

  • Stripe: Payment processing (see Stripe Privacy Policy)
  • Email delivery service: To send PDFs

5. Your Rights (GDPR/CCPA)

You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of your data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of marketing emails

Contact us at support@macrofitcalc.com

6. Data We DON'T Sell

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

7. Cookies

We use minimal cookies for:
