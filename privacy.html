Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 12, 2026

Your Privacy in Plain English: We don't store your personal health data. Your calculator inputs stay on your device. We use cookies for basic site analytics only. No data is sold to third parties.

What Data We Collect

Calculator Inputs (NOT Stored)

When you use our macro calculator, you enter:

  • Age, weight, height
  • Sex and activity level
  • Goal and macro preferences

Important: This data is processed in your browser only. We do NOT store, save, or transmit this information to our servers.

Data We Do Collect

  • Analytics: Anonymous usage data (pages visited, time on site, browser type) via Google Analytics
  • Email Addresses: Only if you voluntarily subscribe to our newsletter
  • Cookies: Small text files for site functionality and analytics

How We Use Your Data

Calculator Data

Your health inputs are processed locally in your browser using JavaScript. Results are calculated on your device and never sent to our servers. If you choose to "Save Results," data is stored in your browser's local storage only.

Email Addresses

If you subscribe to our newsletter:

  • We store your email to send weekly nutrition tips
  • You can unsubscribe anytime
  • We never sell or share your email with third parties
  • Emails are managed through our email service provider (Beehiiv/Mailchimp)

Analytics Data

We use Google Analytics to understand how people use our site:

  • Which pages are most popular
  • How long people spend on the site
  • Geographic location (country/city level only)
  • Device type (mobile/desktop)

This data is anonymous and cannot identify you personally.

Cookies

We use cookies for:

  • Essential Cookies: Remember your preferences (e.g., unit selection)
  • Analytics Cookies: Google Analytics tracking
  • Saved Results: If you click "Save Results," data stays in your browser's local storage

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features may not work properly.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Analytics: Website analytics
  • Beehiiv/Mailchimp: Email newsletter management
  • Amazon Associates: Affiliate product links

These services have their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing them:

  • Google Analytics: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Beehiiv: https://www.beehiiv.com/privacy

Data Security

Since we don't store your health data on our servers, there's nothing to breach. Your calculator inputs:

  • Stay in your browser
  • Are never transmitted to our servers
  • Clear when you close the browser (unless you click "Save")

For data we do collect (emails, analytics), we use industry-standard security practices.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of data we have about you (email only)
  • Delete: Request deletion of your email from our newsletter list
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from emails anytime
  • Opt-out of tracking: Disable cookies or use browser "Do Not Track"

Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Australian Privacy Principles

As an Australian business, we comply with the Privacy Act 1988 and Australian Privacy Principles (APPs).

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
