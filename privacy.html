Privacy Policy

At MacroFitCalc, we believe your health data is your business. Here's exactly what we do (and don't do) with your information.

✔

No Server Storage

Your age, weight, height, and other biometrics are processed locally in your browser. We don't send this data to our servers. Ever. It stays on your device.

✔

Email (Purchases Only)

If you buy a meal plan, we collect your email address to send you the PDF. That's it. We don't sell or share your email with anyone.

✔

Secure Payments

We use Stripe for payment processing. We never see your credit card information. Stripe is PCI-compliant and handles everything securely.

✔

Your Rights (GDPR/CCPA)

You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of your data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of marketing emails (if any)
✔

We Don't Sell Your Data

Period. We don't sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Questions?
